TikTok standout: Special Olympics Minnesota

Minnesota is the undisputed TikTok leader among all U.S. state chapters with ~38,600 followers and over 1.5 million likes — roughly 65× the next confirmed state. Their strategy of short, emotional athlete-story content and event coverage has driven exceptional organic reach, boosted further by hosting the 2026 USA Games. The International account has ~15,900 followers, meaning Minnesota's state chapter actually surpasses the global org on TikTok. Most state chapters have little to no TikTok presence, making this the most open competitive landscape for Louisiana to enter.
