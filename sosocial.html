TikTok standout: Special Olympics Minnesota

Minnesota is the undisputed TikTok leader among all U.S. state chapters — ~38,600 followers and 1.5M+ likes, boosted by hosting the 2026 USA Games. The International account has ~15,900 followers, meaning Minnesota actually surpasses the global org. The vast majority of state chapters have no TikTok presence at all, making this the most open platform for Louisiana to enter and compete.
