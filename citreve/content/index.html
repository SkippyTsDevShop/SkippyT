03 / Content strategy

The authority content engine.

Nobody in Citreve's competitive set has published original research or built a named expert voice. The content plan is built to close that gap before a competitor notices it's open.

Three pillars

One voice, three channels.

Pillar 1

Bar & beverage

Chef POV on garnish craft, freeze-dried vs. dehydrated education, and cocktail program case studies.

Pillar 2

Bakery & QSR

Behind-the-scenes on baking applications, the Honey Dew case study, and food-science credibility content.

Pillar 3

Dirty soda & beverage innovation

Real fruit vs. syrup positioning, trend commentary, and visual, social-native content built for the category's audience.
