TikTok standout: Special Olympics Minnesota

Minnesota leads all U.S. state chapters with ~38,600 followers and 1.5M+ likes — boosted by hosting the 2026 USA Games. The International org has ~15,900 TikTok followers, meaning Minnesota's state chapter surpasses the global account. Most state and international chapters have little to no TikTok presence, making this the most open platform for Louisiana to enter.
